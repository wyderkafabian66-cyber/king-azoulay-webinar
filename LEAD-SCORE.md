# King Azoulay – Lead Score & Setter Playbook

**Max possible score: 25 points.** Use this sheet to quickly read a lead's score and know exactly how to approach the conversation.

---

## The Workflow

```
1. Lead fills the form on site
2. Lead is auto-scored (25-pt max, tiered HOT / WARM / NEUTRAL / COLD)
3. Lead is pushed into bloo.io
4. AI sends the OPENER automatically
5. When the lead replies → setter takes over
6. Setter responds with VOICE MESSAGES (preferred — feels human)
7. Tease the training using the lead's actual answers
8. Drop the "private King access" hook
9. Day before webinar → final push message
```

**Voice messages are the weapon.** They convert way harder than text because they sound real. Use them whenever possible.

## Score Tiers

| **Score** | **Tier** | **What It Means** | **Setter Action** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **20–25** | HOT | High capital, real pain, motivated buyer. Pre-sold. | Setter jumps in within **1 hour** of their reply. Voice messages only. Heavy on the King access hook. |
| **14–19** | WARM | Good fit, some friction. Needs the right angle. | Setter replies same day. Voice messages preferred. Use the tease + King hook normally. |
| **8–13** | NEUTRAL | Possible buyer, lower urgency or lower capital. | Setter replies within 24 hrs. Voice or text. Lighter touch on the hook. |
| **0–7** | COLD | Low capital, low urgency, or brand new. Long sell. | Setter replies when slots open. Text is fine. Focus on the webinar value, not the King hook. |

## The Setter Script Flow

### Step 1 — AI Opener (sent automatically the moment they register)

> Hey [Name], I'm going to be your success coach for King Azoulay's upcoming training. Any questions, just let me know.
>
> By the way — tell me a bit about yourself and what was the main reason you wanted to change?

### Step 2 — Setter Takes Over When They Reply

**Always use voice messages where possible.** Sound natural, not scripted. Use their name. Keep it under 45 seconds.

**Open by tying their reply to one of King's training pillars** (use Q3 / Q4 / Q6 to know which one):

> Yeah man, that's actually one of the things King is going to speak on in the session — so it's definitely going to help you. And you'll walk away with an actual plan for that.

**Pick the tease that matches their Q4 (what they said they need):**

| Their Q4 answer | The tease |
| --- | --- |
| A clear plan / roadmap | "King is literally going to walk through the exact step-by-step on the call." |
| A mentor or coach | "King's going to break down the same system he runs with his private clients." |
| Discipline / accountability | "He's going to show you the structure he uses to actually stay consistent." |
| Belief / confidence | "Wait until you see the stories he tells — people in your exact position." |
| The right network | "There's actually a community piece to this that he's going to introduce on the call." |

### Step 3 — The King Access Hook (drop this on the second or third message)

> The reason I'm talking with you specifically is because you get the chance to speak with King Azoulay or his team after the session — but only if you actively take part.
>
> PS: don't tell anyone I told you this 😉

**When to drop it:**
* HOT (20–25): drop it in the first or second voice message. They're already sold.
* WARM (14–19): drop it after they re-engage in convo. Build a bit of rapport first.
* NEUTRAL (8–13): drop it only if they show clear interest. Don't waste the hook.
* COLD (0–7): don't use the hook. Focus on getting them to show up live first.

### Step 4 — Day-Before Webinar Push (send to every tier)

> Training is tomorrow. You still locked in?
>
> King's got something specific for people who show up from the start — make sure you're on early.

### Step 5 — Day Of Webinar (30 min before start)

> Going live in 30 minutes. Don't miss the first 10 — that's when King drops the framework everything else is built on.

## Voice Message Tips

* **First 5 seconds matter most.** Lead with their name. "Hey [Name], it's [you]..."
* **Keep it under 45 seconds.** Longer messages get skipped.
* **Smile while you record.** It comes through in your voice.
* **Don't read a script.** Talk like a friend who's been there.
* **End with a question.** Makes them reply.
* **Reference their actual answers.** "You said you're trying to [Q6 goal]..." → makes them feel seen.

## Messaging Cadence

| When | What |
| --- | --- |
| **Day 0 (signup)** | AI opener fires automatically. |
| **When they reply** | Setter jumps in — HOT within 1hr, WARM same day, NEUTRAL within 24hr, COLD when slots open. |
| **Day 1–4** | Voice message volley — tease the training, drop the King access hook on the right beat. |
| **2 days before webinar** | "You ready for [date]? Anything you want King to specifically cover?" |
| **Day before webinar** | The exact day-before script above. |
| **30 min before webinar** | The 30-min push above. |
| **Day after webinar** | If they attended → book a call / push the offer. If they skipped → "what got in the way?" + replay link. |

**Reply rule:** Lead replies → respond within 1 hr if HOT, 4 hrs if WARM, 24 hrs if NEUTRAL/COLD.

**Ghost rule:** No reply on a message → second touch 2 days later (different angle). Third touch 5 days later. After 3 unanswered, drop to long-term nurture.
